Manifestos

  • Just Fucking Write Comments Your code isn't self-documenting. It never was. Stop lying to yourself.
  • Just Fucking Say What You Want Stop sending "hi" and waiting. Your fake politeness is wasting everyone's time.
  • Just Fucking Read the Error Message The answer is right there. You just didn't read it.
  • Just Fucking Stop Overengineering You don't need that abstraction. You need to ship.
  • Just Fucking Send the Error Report Send it to the developer. Stop hoarding it like a secret.
